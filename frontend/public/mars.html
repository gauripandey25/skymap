Mars

Details

  • Name: Mars
  • Type: Terrestrial planet
  • Diameter: 6,779 km
  • Orbit: 687 Earth days
  • Distance from Sun: 227.9 million km
  • Notable Features: Red color due to iron oxide, large volcanoes, and canyon systems

History

Mars has been observed since ancient times and was named after the Roman god of war due to its red color, which was associated with blood and battle.

Legends

In mythology, Mars was the god of war. Its red hue made it a symbol of conflict and aggression in many ancient cultures.

Interesting Facts

  • Mars has the largest volcano in the Solar System, Olympus Mons.
  • Its atmosphere is thin and composed mostly of carbon dioxide.
  • There are signs that liquid water may have flowed on its surface in the past.
