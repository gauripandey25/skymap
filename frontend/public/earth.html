Earth

Details

  • Name: Earth
  • Type: Terrestrial planet
  • Diameter: 12,742 km
  • Orbit: 365.25 days
  • Distance from Sun: 149.6 million km
  • Notable Features: Diverse ecosystems, liquid water, atmosphere with oxygen

History

Earth has been home to life for approximately 3.5 billion years. It formed about 4.5 billion years ago and has undergone significant geological and climatic changes over its history.

Legends

In various mythologies, Earth is often personified as a goddess or mother figure, reflecting its role as the nurturing and sustaining force of life. In Greek mythology, Gaia was the primordial goddess of the Earth.

Interesting Facts

  • Earth is the only planet in the Solar System with liquid water on its surface.
  • About 71% of Earth's surface is covered by water, with the remaining 29% consisting of land.
  • Earth's atmosphere is composed of 78% nitrogen, 21% oxygen, and trace amounts of other gases.
