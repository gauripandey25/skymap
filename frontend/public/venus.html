Venus

Details

  • Name: Venus
  • Type: Terrestrial planet
  • Diameter: 12,104 km
  • Orbit: 225 Earth days
  • Distance from Sun: 108.2 million km
  • Notable Features: Thick atmosphere, high surface temperatures

History

Venus has been observed since ancient times and was named after the Roman goddess of love and beauty due to its bright appearance.

Legends

In mythology, Venus is associated with love and beauty. Its bright, golden hue made it a symbol of femininity and allure in many ancient cultures.

Interesting Facts

  • Venus rotates in the opposite direction to most other planets.
  • Its atmosphere is primarily composed of carbon dioxide, with clouds of sulfuric acid.
