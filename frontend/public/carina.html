Carina

Details

  • Name: Carina
  • Type: Constellation
  • Location: Southern sky
  • Notable Features: Canopus, the Carina Nebula

History

Carina was once part of the larger constellation Argo Navis, which represented the ship Argo from Greek mythology. It was later divided into several constellations, with Carina representing the keel.

Legends

The constellation Carina represents the keel of the Argo, the ship used by Jason and the Argonauts in their quest for the Golden Fleece.

Interesting Facts

  • Canopus, the second-brightest star in the night sky, is located in Carina.
  • The Carina Nebula is one of the most massive star-forming regions in the Milky Way galaxy.
  • Carina is visible primarily from the southern hemisphere and is best observed during the summer months in the southern latitudes.
