Mercury

Details

  • Name: Mercury
  • Type: Terrestrial planet
  • Diameter: 4,880 km
  • Orbit: 88 Earth days
  • Distance from Sun: 57.9 million km
  • Notable Features: Extreme temperature variations, heavily cratered surface

History

Mercury has been known since ancient times and was named after the Roman messenger god due to its swift orbit around the Sun.

Legends

In mythology, Mercury is often depicted as a swift messenger, embodying speed and agility. Its fast orbit gave it a reputation for being the fastest celestial object in the sky.

Interesting Facts

  • Mercury has no moons.
  • Its surface temperature can range from -173°C to 427°C.
  • It has a very thin atmosphere, mostly composed of oxygen, sodium, and hydrogen.
